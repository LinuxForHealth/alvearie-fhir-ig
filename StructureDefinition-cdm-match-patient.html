Resource Profile: CDM Match Patient

Official URL: http://ibm.com/fhir/cdm/StructureDefinition/cdm-match-patient Version: 7.0.0
Active as of 2022-09-06 Computable Name: CDMMatchPatient

Match patient record created when matched patients are found, containing links to the source patient resources that were matched

Usage:
